About

La Barbera is an Italian fashion house based in London founded by Angelo La Barbera. He was born and raised in Sicily, moved to Milan and 4 years after he moved to London.

Angelo graduated from Ba at Politecnico di Milano in Fashion Design and Ma in Fashion Womenswear at London College of Fashion while also working for Sharon Wauchob. In 2020 his collection is shown during London Fashion Week.

The brand focuses on the analysis of the relationship between objects and people. This intangible affection, this synergy between object and subject as a process of mutual becoming is the core of the brand research.Nurturing a new definition of materialism based on emotions and affect.

The aim is to create outcomes that can be treasured and are a constant reminder of the story that is behind them. The shortened product lifecycle can be tackled using this approach to promote durability.

Craftsmanship and Italian tradition are at the centre of the brand values and combined with constant research of materials and new techniques to create luxury products that set aside from the fast pace of today’s fashion system.

The brand researches new perspectives to reinvent people’s closet, changing our attitude towards the choice of garments we have to wear everyday. Being a result of the relationship we have to each garment and the feeling we experience wearing them.
